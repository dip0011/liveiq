# LiveIQ - The AI Front Desk

The AI front desk built to grow Medical, Fitness, & Wellness franchises. LiveIQ handles all calls + texts, books appointments, and grows your business autonomously.

---

## About LiveIQ

LiveIQ is an AI-powered front desk solution designed for healthcare and wellness businesses. It deploys intelligent AI agents that act as receptionists, analysts, and marketers, handling client communications, booking appointments, running campaigns, and driving revenue growth 24/7 without human intervention.

### What LiveIQ Does

- **Handles Calls & Texts**: AI agents answer every inbound call and text message, ensuring no client goes unanswered. Supports 20+ languages.
- **Books Appointments**: Schedules directly into your existing EMR/CRM and booking systems via deep integrations with 100+ platforms including Hubspot, Mindbody, Boulevard, and more.
- **Converts Leads**: Automatically follows up with new leads from your CRM using intelligent sales tactics to get them booked.
- **Fills Cancellations**: Detects cancelled slots and instantly reaches out to waitlisted clients to fill gaps in your calendar.
- **Re-activates Past Clients**: Runs targeted campaigns to bring back lapsed clients with personalized promos based on their history.
- **Sells Memberships**: Identifies frequent clients and upgrades them to membership plans through conversational upselling.
- **Collects Reviews & Feedback**: Automates post-visit review requests and feedback collection, writing directly into your EMR.
- **Intelligent Upselling**: AI-recommended upsells based on client history and service patterns.
- **Omni-Channel Communication**: Engages clients across phone calls, SMS, and chat from a unified platform.
- **Seamless Human Handoff**: Smoothly transfers conversations to human staff when needed, with full context preserved.

## Agency Concierge

LiveIQ also offers a dedicated **Agency Concierge** product for marketing agencies and business consultancies serving the wellness industry.

### What the Agency Concierge Does

- **Speed to Lead**: Immediately reaches out to all leads over call, text, or email
- **Sales Expertise**: Qualifies, nurtures, and handles objections like a human
- **Direct Booking**: Schedules directly into clients' booking systems
- **Attribution & Analytics**: Maps attribution, ROI, and LTV through direct integrations
- **Commission-Based Pricing**: Supports commission models, automatically charging clients using revenue data
- **Automatic Upselling**: Upsells high-ticket items to increase revenue per client

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- Node.js 20+
- npm

### Installation

```bash
npm install
```

### Development

```bash
npm run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) for the landing page and [http://localhost:3000/agency](http://localhost:3000/agency) for the agency page.

### Production Build

```bash
npm run build
npm start
```
